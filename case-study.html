THE CHALLENGE

Neon//Dreams came to us with a problem: they were invisible in a crowded market. Despite having revolutionary streetwear designs, their brand identity was forgettable. They needed to stop being another face in the crowd.

02

THE INSIGHT

We discovered that their target audience — Gen Z in Tokyo, New York, and Berlin — craved authenticity above all else. They could spot a inauthentic brand from a mile away. The answer wasn't to add more — it was to strip away everything unnecessary.

03

THE SOLUTION

We developed a brand system based on controlled chaos. The visual language combines brutalist typography with unexpected color moments, creating a tension between order and disorder that reflects the brand's DNA.
