THE COLLECTIVE

WE ARE
NOIR//SYSTEM

A global creative collective operating at the intersection of art, design, and culture.

NOT A STUDIO.
A MOVEMENT.

NOIR//SYSTEM was born in 2019 when five creative souls from different corners of the world decided that the design industry needed a wake-up call.

We are not a traditional agency. We are an art collective that happens to do branding, web design, and art direction. Our work is our art. Our clients are our collaborators.
